Fitness Routine for Beginners

Stay healthy with this simple workout plan!

Warm-Up Strength Training Cardio Benefits

Warm-Up Exercises

Start with a 5-10 minute warm-up to prepare your body:

  • Jumping Jacks: 1 minute
  • High Knees: 1 minute
  • Arm Circles: 30 seconds forward/backward
  • Bodyweight Squats: 15 reps

Full Body Strength Training

Perform 3 sets of each exercise:

  • Push-Ups: 10-15 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats: 12-15 reps
  • Plank: Hold for 30-45 seconds
  • Dumbbell Rows: 10 reps per arm
  • Lunges: 10 reps per leg

Cardio & Core

Choose a 20-minute cardio option:

  • Brisk Walking or Jogging
  • Jump Rope
  • Cycling
  • Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds x 4 sets

Benefits of Regular Exercise

  • Improves overall physical fitness
  • Boosts mental health and reduces stress
  • Strengthens your muscles and bones
  • Enhances energy levels and focus
